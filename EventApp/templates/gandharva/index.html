About Gandharva

Gandharva is a techno-cultural fest organized by students and held every year at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology. There are various technical events and workshops spanning various engineering domains and also exciting cultural events that take place. It's a great platform to increase your skills and showcase your talents. Do join us this for this fun, learning and rewarding experience.
